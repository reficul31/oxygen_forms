Get started by Registering

Get started by registering on thw website. Fill in the details asked and get started with your awesome journey of creating dynamic forms. We will never share your information with others, you can trust us.

Login and Design the Form

Login in to your account and get started by designing custom forms by goind to design. We support text fields, radio buttons, checkboxes etc. We will keep the information private and the data entered in your custom forms will never be accessible to any third party application.

Distribute and see the information

Once the form is created look at all the forms created on the home screen under my forms. You can either click on link which will take you to page where the form is currently being hosted or you an click on the settings icon to see all the entries in the form.
